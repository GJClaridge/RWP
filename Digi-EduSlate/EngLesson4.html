Lesson 4. Days of the Week.

Lesson Overview

In this lesson you will learn the days of the week in English.

By the end of this lesson...

You should be able to say the days of the week and understand the order they are in.

Days of the Week

There are 7 Days of the week. The days of the week are as follows:

Monday : Day 1 (first day) of the Week
Tuesday : Day 2 (second day) of the Week
Wednesday : Day 3 (third day) of the Week
Thursday : Day 4 (fourth day) of the Week
Friday: Day 5 (fifth day) of the Week
These days can also be known as weekdays.

Saturday : Day 6 (sixth day) of the Week
Sunday : Day 7 (seventh day) of the Week.
These days can be know as the weekend.
