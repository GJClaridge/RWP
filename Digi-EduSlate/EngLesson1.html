Lesson 1. The Alphabet.

Lesson Overview

In this lesson you will learn the English Alphabet. This includes knowing which ones are vowels and consonants.

By the end of this lesson...

You should know the letters of the English Alphabet. You should know which ones are vowels and which ones are consonants. By using the related video, you could learn how to say each letter.

The Alphabet

There are 26 letters in the English Alphabet. They can be written as capitals (BIG) and lower case (small). Below is the alphabet in order.

Aa Bb Cc Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz

The Vowels

There are 5 letters in the English Alphabet that are vowels. They are:

Aa Ee Ii Oo Uu

The Consonants

There are 21 letters in the English Alphabet that are Consonants. They are:

Bb Cc Dd Ff Gg Hh Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz

Summary of lesson

This lesson aimed to teach you to a basic level the English Alphabet. From this lesson and the video you should be able to say and regonise the letters of the English Alphabet as well know which letters are consonants and which are vowels.
