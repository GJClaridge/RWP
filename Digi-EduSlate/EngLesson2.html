Lesson 2. Numbers.

Lesson Overview

In this lesson you will learn numbers from 1 - 100.

By the end of this lesson...

You should understand the numbers from 1 - 100 and be able to say them.

Numbers 1-20

The first numbers are 1- 20. Below they are listed with their names:

1 - One | 2 - Two | 3 - Three | 4 - Four | 5 - Five | 6 - Six | 7 - Seven | 8 - Eight | 9 - Nine | 10 - Ten
11 - Eleven | 12 - Twelve | 13 - Thirteen | 14 - Fourteen | 15 - Fifteen | 16 - Sixteen | 17 - Seventeen | 18 - Eighteen | 19 - Nineteen | 20 - Twenty

Numbers 21-99

Beyond 20, there is a pattern up to 100. Each number, such as 20 and 30, have their own names, but numbers such as 21 is the name for 20 and the name for 1, therefore Twenty one. The names for each ten is shown below.

20 - Twenty | 30 - Thirty | 40 - Fourty | 50 - Fivety | 60 - Sixty | 70 - Seventy | 80 - Eighty | 90 - Ninety

Number 100

The number 100 is called One Hundred. Numbers beyond 100 use the numbers as above, with one hundred in front. So 101 is one hundred and one.
