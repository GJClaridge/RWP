Important!

If there is a emergency, do NOT risk your own wellbeing or that of others. Wherever you're Digi-EduSlate is, do not worry about it. The most important thing in a time of emergency is that you find safety. The Digi-EduSlate aims to teach you what to do in such a situation, so you can respond to the situation without it.
