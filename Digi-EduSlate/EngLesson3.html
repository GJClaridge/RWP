Lesson 3. Introductions, Greetings and Goodbyes.

Lesson Overview

In this lesson you will learn how to introduce yourself, as well as greet someone and say goodbye.

By the end of this lesson...

You should be able to introduce yourself to someone. You should be able to greet them and say goodbye.

Introductions

An introduction is telling someone about yourself. Some phrases to introduce yourself are:

What is your name? - My name is (name).
Where are you from? - I come from (place).

Greetings

Greetings are how you start talking to someone. Some common greetings are:

Hello - Hi (if you know the person)
Good Morning
Good Afternoon
Good Evening

Goodbyes

A goodbye is a way to end talking to someone politely. Some common goodbyes are:

Goodbye - Bye (if you know the person)
Goodnight
See you - See you later
